About Team - Meet

Team meet was dreamt up by web developers and a network tech. In 2015, 4 students took a class in which they had to dream up, build, and test a system that could be implemented in the real world. The results of this is the website you are now on. Team Meet is a revolutionary way for teams to come together and share ideas, share files, and talk in real time in one place. Nowhere else does a website combine these ideas into one, easy to use, package.
